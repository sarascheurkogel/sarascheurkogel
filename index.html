about this site

Hi! Welcome to my portfolio website :)

I am a really big fan of the weird 2000s websites, I took big inspiration from them for my portfolio website.

The creativity with colors, shapes and styles is what made those websites all feel very unique to their creator. Which is exactly the goal of a portfolio, showing who I am and what I can do as a designer.

For my particular process in web design I always start off with moodboards. I decided on a retro experience, like in website windows (to honor the 2000s websites). For the colorscheme I went with my favorite colors, a gentle pink/green combination.

To allow for full creativity and as a learning opportunity I decided to code the website myself, rather than using a web builder.

I hope you enjoy my Portfolio :)

CMD Fails

In collaboration with Rochee Favery, Lennart Driedijk, Victor Quaedvlieg
For the new students joining CMD, it is incredibly difficult to get used to a design process with iterations.

Through interviews with new students we found that they struggled with making mistakes, it made them feel insecure, unmotivated and like a failure.

Through testing we ended up with a design to make mistakes feel less heavy and more normalised.
